Things to do in Varna

Attractions:

Aquarium

Varna Aquarium is located in The Sea Garden and is one Varna’s best known attractions. It is a center popularizing Black Sea flora and fauna as well as Black sea hydrologic characteristics. The exposition shows representatives of all fish groups in the Black sea. Best presented are the ones from The Mediterranean inhabited the Black sea through the Bosphorus. Black sea fauna consists of around 140 species.

Sea Garden

The Sea Garden is the largest and best known public park in Varna, also said to be the largest landscaped park on the Balkans. Located along the city's coast on the Black Sea, it is an important tourist attraction and a national monument of landscape architecture. In the middle of the 19th century the Sea Garden present day area was a bare field outside the city limits. In 1862 a small garden was arranged on the orders of the city's Ottoman mayor. After the Liberation of Bulgaria in 1878 mayor Mihail Koloni suggested the arrangement of a city garden and a seaside park in 1881, and despite suspicions a small sum was granted. As a result the Sea Garden was expanded to 26,000 m² and further developed according to the plan of French engineer Martinice.

Restaurants:

Fish restaurant ship "SIRIUS" Varna

Welcome aboard the Sirius restaurant ship. The unique frigate from the 13th century has been converted into a specialized fish restaurant with 150 seats. The Sirius ship anchored on land in 1991. and since then until now it has been welcoming year-round locals and foreign guests. It is located on the seashore in the resort of Sveti Konstantin and Elena and has the fame of the oldest and classiest fish restaurant near Varna. The interior of the ship consists of two levels where the four dining rooms and bar are located with dark wood furniture, sea charts and lanterns.

Happy bar and grill

Happy Center Varna opens its doors in 2012. and from then until now welcomes its guests in the heart of our Sea Capital. Located on Sevastopol Square, emblematic for the people of Varna, the restaurant has an indoor area as well as a summer garden. At Happy you can enjoy stylish and modern design as well as a delicious variety of food. Thanks to the company's uncompromising standards, you can count on a quick business lunch with us and a relaxed and pleasant dinner in a friendly atmosphere.

Shopping:

Delta Planet Mall

This is the largest new shopping center in Bulgaria with an expanded built-up area of ​​over 120,000 sq. m, of which over 40,000 sq. m is commercial space. Delta Planet Mall is fully subordinated to the modern retail concept - a precisely balanced mix between shopping, entertainment and leisure. The center offers residents of Varna and guests of the city over 4,000 square meters of entertainment territory, which houses the largest cinema outside Sofia - with 12 cinemas, and the only 4DX hall in Varna. More than 140 stores form the shopping area of ​​the modern shopping and entertainment center.

Varna Mall

Varna Mall is one of the first shopping centers in Varna and the region, providing a variety of opportunities for leisure, shopping and entertainment suitable for every age group. On the territory of Varna Mall, goods of well-known Bulgarian and world-famous brands are offered, as well as a number of attractions, turning the stay in the complex into a real experience. In the shops of the shopping center you will find a wide selection of sports, casual and formal wear by New Yorker, Mercari, AVVA, MUDO, Teodor, Just Unique, Vogex, shoes and accessories by Geox, Estil and Art-93. You can choose a fine piece of jewelry from Assos and Krikorian diamonds as a gift, dress your children from head to toe at Kinderland and Carnival Kids.
